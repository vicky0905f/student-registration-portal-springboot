About us:

Data Sticks is a student registration portal designed to streamline the process of collecting and managing student information. Our platform provides an easy-to-use interface for students to register their details, including their name, age, department, and CGPA. With Data Sticks, educational institutions can efficiently maintain a comprehensive database of student records, ensuring accurate and up-to-date information for administrative purposes. Our goal is to simplify the registration process while enhancing data management for educational institutions.
